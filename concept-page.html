Welcome to Jungle Joes Travels and adventures!


Every year we set up exotic travels to 4 carefully selected destinations. You can sign up for one, to or three weeks at the time. We organise the travel, activities, meals and accommodation for your entire stay. All destination offers a variety of activities spanning from climbing and hiking, to diving and refting. Alle destinations are equipped with symbols indicating which activities are available at your chosen destination - have a look at the table below for explanations.


Travels are all inclusive with luxuryous sleepovers at base camp. You can choose to sign up for any activity at being organised at your adventure destination - just make sure to show up fit, up well equipped and prepared. Each destination comes with a suggested packing list.


Prices are fixed, based on one, two or three weeks participation. Our adventurers are joining us from all over the world. We offer to customize you travel from door to door - just get in touch and well give you the best prize available wherever you are travelling from. We can also handle visa enqiuries and assist with packing lists and vaccination programs for your country.
